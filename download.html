Frequently Asked Questions

Is NoteVault free to use? +

Yes! NoteVault's core features are completely free. We offer a Pro plan for power users who want unlimited cloud sync, premium templates, and priority support.

Can I use NoteVault on multiple devices? +

Absolutely! With Pro, you can sync your vault across unlimited devices using your preferred cloud provider. The free version works perfectly offline on a single device.

Is my data secure? +

Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We never access your notes, and our core engine is open-source for complete transparency.

Can I import from other apps? +

Yes! NoteVault supports importing from Obsidian, Notion, Evernote, Apple Notes, and standard Markdown files. Our built-in import wizard makes it seamless.

Do you offer refunds? +

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all Pro plans. If you're not satisfied, just contact our support team for a full refund.
