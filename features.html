Bi-directional Links

Connect your notes seamlessly and discover hidden relationships.

NoteVault's bi-directional linking creates a web of knowledge that mirrors how your brain works. Link any two notes together, and NoteVault automatically creates a backlink so you can always trace the connections.

Interactive Linking Interface
  • Automatic backlink detection and panel
  • Quick linking with [[double bracket]] syntax
  • Unlinked mentions detection
  • Orphan notes analysis

Markdown Support

Write in plain text with full formatting power.

Write your notes using Markdown — the universal language of the web. Full support for headers, lists, tables, code blocks, blockquotes, and more. Your notes are always portable and never locked into a proprietary format.

# This is a heading
This is **bold** and this is *italic*.

- Item two
- Nested item

Canvas Mode

A free-form space to think visually.

Canvas gives you an infinite workspace to arrange notes, images, and ideas. Drag notes onto the canvas, connect them with arrows, and think freely without linear constraints.

Free-form Layout

Place notes anywhere on an infinite canvas. Zoom in and out to see details or the big picture.

Visual Connections

Draw arrows between notes to show relationships and build visual knowledge maps.

Graph View

See your entire knowledge base as an interactive network.

The graph view renders your notes as a beautiful, interactive network. See clusters of related ideas, discover orphaned notes, and understand the structure of your thinking at a glance.

  • Force-directed graph layout
  • Filter by tags, folders, or search
  • Interactive exploration and navigation
  • Highlight paths between notes

Plugin Ecosystem

Extend NoteVault infinitely with community plugins.

Our powerful plugin API lets developers create extensions that integrate deeply with NoteVault. Thousands of plugins are available in the community marketplace.

  • Rich plugin API with full access control
  • 4,000+ community plugins available
  • Support for JavaScript, TypeScript, and WebAssembly
  • Easy publishing to the community marketplace

Local-First Privacy

Your notes are yours. Always.

NoteVault stores your notes locally on your device by default. We don't read, sell, or share your data. End-to-end encryption is available for synced data.

  • Notes stored locally on your device
  • End-to-end encryption for cloud sync
  • No telemetry or analytics
  • Open source core available on GitHub
  • Export to plain text anytime

Real-time Collaboration

Work together seamlessly with your team.

Share notes and work together in real-time. See cursors move, edit simultaneously, and leave comments for your teammates.

Advanced Search

Find anything instantly across your entire vault.

Powerful full-text search across all your notes. Filter by tag, date, folder, or content type. Regex support for complex queries.

Themes

Beautiful dark and light themes for any mood.

Choose from dozens of beautiful themes or create your own. NoteVault supports CSS-based theming with full customization.
